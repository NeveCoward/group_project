About Us

This website was created by two students on the Sheffield University Code First: Girls Course. You can get to know us a bit more on this page or follow our progress on Github.

Neve Coward

Neve is a BA Languages and Cultures student, studying French, Spanish and Russian at the University of Sheffield. She became more aware of environmental issues since coming to university, and living in a city for the first time! She loves Sheffield and how green it is and wants to keep it that way for others to enjoy in the future.

Fenella Snudden

Fenella is an MA Architecture student at the University of Sheffield with an interest in sustainable design. She is interested in Sheffield’s industrial heritage and its potential to reinvent and become a green urban centre. She loves Sheffield’s green spaces, landscape and proximity to the Peak district.
