Welcome to Urban Sheffield


Urban Sheffield is a tool to help everyone understand the environmental situation in Sheffield, summarising key facts covering waste, green spaces, trees, pollution and energy sources, and regional news including what action is being taken. This interactive map spotlights the most polluted areas in the city, as well as the tram routes available for those wanting to contribute to a greener Sheffield.
